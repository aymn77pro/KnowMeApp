## Over View
Application store each user information and social accounts. the Main idea of the application is to make it easier for individuals to save their friends and family information .

## What i use in this app
- MVVM.

- Clean architecture .

- Fire Store .

- Fire Base Storge .

- Sing up/in by google.
 
- Use google account image .

- Count how many friend and family . 

- Setting for change app layout .

- QR Code to save user Information .

- Search by name .

- Location for your friend .

- Choose any image you want .

- Read QR codes .
